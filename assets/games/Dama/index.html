How to Play Filipino Dama

  • Play on the 32 dark squares only (standard 8×8 board)

  • Reach the last row to promote to Dama (King)

Filipino Dama Rules

Board: 8×8 checkerboard, using only 32 dark squares. Each player has 12 pieces on their first 3 rows.

Movement (Men):

  • Move diagonally forward only (toward opponent)
  • One square at a time to empty dark squares
  • Cannot move backward or sideways

Capturing:

  • Jump over adjacent opponent piece diagonally forward
  • Land on empty square immediately after
  • Capturing is mandatory
  • Must capture maximum number of pieces available
  • Multiple captures allowed with direction changes

Dama (King):

  • Promoted when reaching the last row (end of turn)
  • Moves any distance diagonally in all directions
  • Captures by "flying jump" - can jump from any distance
  • Must stop on square immediately after captured piece

Winning: Capture all opponent pieces or block all moves.
